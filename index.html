Process

01

Brief & Direction

Understand the product, audience, offer, campaign goal, brand identity, and platform use.

02

Visual Planning

Define the scene, mood, composition, color direction, and image purpose before production.

03

AI Generation

Create multiple visual directions using controlled prompts, references, and iterative refinement.

04

Correction & Quality Control

Review AI-generated outputs for product accuracy, visual defects, deformation, inconsistent details, incorrect branding, and other common AI issues. Correct or regenerate assets as needed to ensure the image is realistic, controlled, and usable.

05

Selection & Refinement

Choose the strongest outputs and improve product clarity, visual consistency, composition, and overall polish.

06

Layout & Format Adaptation

Prepare assets for product listings, ads, landing pages, email, social media, and ecommerce formats.

07

Final Delivery

Export polished, platform-ready images organized by use case and format.

A product image is not decoration.

It is the first promise a brand makes before the customer ever touches the item.

The moment a customer opens a package is the moment your product image either earns their trust or loses it. That gap — between what they saw and what arrived — is where brands are built or quietly abandoned.

My goal is not to disguise products. It is to surface their value.

To make them easier to trust.

Easier to understand.

Easier to choose.

Not by changing the product, but by making the truth feel more desirable.

Because a beautiful image that misrepresents the product is not strong product photography. It is a delayed return, a disappointed customer, and a brand promise quietly broken.
